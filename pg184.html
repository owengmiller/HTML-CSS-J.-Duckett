For a one-year period in November 2010, the Marguame Genichiro-Inokuma Museum of Contemporary Art (MIMOCA) will host a cycle of four Hiroshi Sugimoto exhibitions.

Each will showcase works by the artist thematically contextualized under the headings "Science," "Architechture," "History" and "Relgion" so as to present a comprehensive panorama of the artist's oevure.

Hours: 10:00 - 18:00 (No admittance after 17:30)
