Scrambled Eggs:

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs of butter
  • 2tbs of cream

Method:

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan on medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted, add the eggs and cream.
  4. Using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist, turn off the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
